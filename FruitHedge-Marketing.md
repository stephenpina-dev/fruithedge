## Objection Handling

**"Isn't this just another personality test?"**
No. Personality tests diagnose and leave. FruitHedge measures, diagnoses, AND coaches ‚Äî with time-aware advice that changes based on when you use it.

**"Why not just use ChatGPT?"**
ChatGPT hallucinates. It makes things up. FruitHedge has 980 pre-written messages ‚Äî every word chosen by a human. No probability. No mistakes.

**"How is this different from a horoscope?"**
Horoscopes are generic ("Taurus will have a good week"). FruitHedge measures your actual inputs and matches them to specific patterns. The advice is earned by your data, not assigned by your birthday.

**"Why should I trust the formulas?"**
The three laws are based on established research: autonomy (Self-Determination Theory), resonance (parasocial connection research), and craft intensity (flow state research). The composite uses geometric mean to ensure balance.

**"What if I don't like my results?"**
Then you learned something. The tool doesn't tell you what you want to hear ‚Äî it tells you what you need to hear. That's the point.
